Our Comprehensive Protection Solutions

Service Entrance Protection

First line of defense against external power surges entering your facility. Our service entrance protectors are designed to handle the largest surges before they can damage your equipment.

Panel-Level Protection

Secondary protection at your main electrical panels to stop any surges that make it past the service entrance. These devices provide whole-building protection for your electrical system.

Point-of-Use Protection

Final defense for your most sensitive electronics. Our point-of-use protectors are installed directly at the equipment to ensure the highest level of protection for your valuable devices.
