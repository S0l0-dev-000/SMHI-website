How Power Factor Correction Works

Power factor correction improves your electrical system's efficiency by balancing the reactive power in your facility's electrical network, particularly for inductive loads like motors and transformers.

  • Balances inductive loads with capacitor banks
  • Reduces reactive power charges on your utility bill
  • Improves voltage regulation and system capacity

Ideal for facilities with:

  • Industrial manufacturing plants
  • HVAC systems and chillers
  • Facilities with large motor loads
