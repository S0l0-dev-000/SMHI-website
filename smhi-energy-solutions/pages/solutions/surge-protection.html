Why Surge Protection Matters

Electrical surges can cause significant damage to your equipment and lead to costly downtime. Our surge protection solutions provide comprehensive defense against power anomalies, ensuring your operations continue uninterrupted.

  • Protects sensitive equipment like VFDs, LED drivers, and control systems
  • Prevents failures from voltage spikes, switching transients, and lightning strikes
  • Extends the life of your electrical infrastructure and connected equipment

Common Surge Sources:

  • Lightning strikes and utility switching
  • Large motor starts and stops
  • Faulty wiring and power grid issues
  • Electronic equipment cycling on/off

  • Panel-level SPDs for critical loads and subpanels
  • Point-of-use protection for sensitive electronics
  • Ideal when paired with LED, VFD, and EMS systems
